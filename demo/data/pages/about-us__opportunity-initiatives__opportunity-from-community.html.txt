## CommBank Staff Foundation
Our employees are committed to driving positive change within their communities. We have a long history of employee giving through our people’s charity, CommBank Staff Foundation. Every dollar our people donate is matched by the Group.

## Volunteering
Our large, diverse organisation has employees with a range of skills that we donate to help not-for-profit organisations, community groups and social enterprises achieve their goals. CommBank employees can volunteer their expertise through skilled volunteering and secondment programs.

## Disaster and crisis support
We’re here to support our customers and communities in times of need. We activate our Emergency Assistance for customers and businesses affected by bushfires, floods and storms, as well as activate community organisation support.
