Title: Smart Integrated EFTPOS terminal

## Wired for reliable connectivity
Say goodbye to lost signals, dropouts or charging issues. Smart Integrated uses a wired broadband connection to stay online even during peak hours. No Wi-Fi interference, no mobile signal issues – just consistent, dependable performance for large volume payments.

## Quick & secure payments
Get paid faster Same-day settlement with a linked CommBank Business Transaction Account , or five-day settlement with another bank. 1
Streamline your checkout Easily integrates with 500+ POS systems for faster checkouts, fewer manual errors and automated reconciliation. 2
Make payments effortless Features surcharging 3 and tipping, with an accessible keypad designed to make payments simple and inclusive. Protect your business & customers Bank-grade security and fraud protection to keep you safe.

## Accepts all major payment types

## Keep payments flowing
Touchscreens can be tricky with wet hands. Smart Integrated has both a touchscreen and buttons to help keep payments flowing – perfect for pools, busy bars or venues, and people who prefer a physical keypad.

## How to apply
To apply for a Smart Integrated terminal, you'll need:
To be registered with ASIC
An Australian Business Number (ABN), business structure and registered address
Details of every director, partner or trustee depending on your business structure

## Apply online
A device running Windows 10 or above with an available USB-A port (e.g. your POS system or connected computer)
A compatible POS system
A CommBank Business Transaction Account 8

## Call us
You'll need to call us if you:
Need more than 5 terminals or stands
Already have a Smart Integrated terminal and want another
Take more than $250,000 per year in card transactions
Need to receive settlement funds in a non-CommBank account
Need a Smart Integrated terminal for a business in the betting or transport industries
Have a CommBank Relationship Manager
