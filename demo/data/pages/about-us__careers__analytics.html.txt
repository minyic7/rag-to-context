## Data Management
Great data management helps our people and customers have trust in our data. As part of our data management team, you’ll support the bank to understand data, its quality and how it’s used, enabling teams to make better business decisions and drive customer value. You’ll also make sure our critical data has strong governance and sufficient controls in place to manage risks across the data lifecycle. As we move towards democratising data, you’ll help drive high levels of data literacy across all consumers in the business.

## Analytics Engineering & Platform Management
Our job is to build and manage technology platforms and systems designed to extract and consume data. As part of this team, you’ll build simplified and scalable cloud-based data platforms, making sure they’re as advanced and reliable as possible so the business can seamlessly access complex datasets. The systems this team builds and manages are critical to delivering insights and powering AI to help improve our customer experience.

My favourite part of my role is that I learn something new almost every day. Data science is a broad discipline with new tools and techniques emerging all the time, so there is always more to learn!”
