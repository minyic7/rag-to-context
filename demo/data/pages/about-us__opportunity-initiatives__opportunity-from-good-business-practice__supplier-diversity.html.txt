Title: Supplier diversity - CommBank

## Program Overview
CBA is Australia’s leading financial services institution, with operations across the world. To make this global operation run smoothly, the Bank has a complex and diverse supply chain – with approximately 4,000 suppliers. Our purpose is to build a brighter future for all. To achieve this we aim to grow the breadth and depth of projects awarded to diverse suppliers. We also look to support inclusion and diversity initiatives in all our suppliers. This not only multiplies the impact of our program, it ensures diverse suppliers are not locked out of CBA’s supply chain due to pre-existing supplier relationships. CBA has a target to increase its supply chain spend with First Nations suppliers by 10% year-on-year. Diverse suppliers are those that are 50% or more diverse owned and controlled, are profit-for-purpose or are certified with a relevant not-for-profit organisation.

## Supply Nation partnership
CBA is proud to be a Supply Nation Red Ochre Partner, the highest possible level. As part of the partnership we are: Platinum sponsors of the Supply Nation Gala Awards Dinner, celebrating success in the Indigenous business sector Principal sponsor of the JumpStart platform, connecting skilled volunteers to task-based needs of Indigenous businesses

## Are you interested in working with CBA?
Our supplier diversity program looks to work with Indigenous owned businesses, women owned businesses, social enterprises and disability enterprises. We define Indigenous businesses as those that are registered with Supply Nation.
