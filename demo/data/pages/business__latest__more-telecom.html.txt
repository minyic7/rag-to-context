## Frequently asked questions

## Why is CommBank working with More?
CommBank is supporting businesses to manage rising cost pressures and is working with More to provide exclusive offers on More's nbn™, phone and mobile plans.

## Can I keep my business phone or mobile numbers?
Yes, you can transfer your existing phone or mobile numbers to More to ensure there's no disruption to your business. Visit the More website to find out more.

## Will there be any disruptions when switching to More?
More provides CommBank Business customers with a personal Connection Manager, to manage the planning and delivery of a smooth and seamless transfer.

## Can I transfer my existing contract to More³?
Transferring to More may trigger your existing provider to charge a cancellation fee if you're still in contract. However, the savings with More may outweigh these cancellation fees. We recommend checking with your current provider prior to switching to avoid any surprises.

## Can I keep my existing phone system?
Possibly, many IP phone handsets work with More’s system and can be re-configured. However, most businesses take up the included hardware on a 24-month contract for a seamless connection process. Your personal Connection Manager will help you through the process.

## Can I get connected to the nbn®?
nbn® stands for National Broadband Network. Most Australians can access the internet from their homes and businesses with nbn®.
The nbn® rollout is largely complete with most addresses now able to connect to the network. You can use More's address checker to see if it's available in your area.

## Can I use my own modem?
For a seamless set-up, it is recommended to purchase a pre-configured More modem/router – which happens to be included in the More 24-month Business contract 2 . However, if you’re tech-savvy and keen to bring your own modem you certainly can, and where possible More will try to help you with configuration. Check out More’s hardware options here .

## What plan does my business need?
We understand that circumstances can change in your business (e.g. Covid impacts over the last 2 years), which is why we offer a number of flexible options to understand your usual business performance, including the consideration of one year financial statements. Speak to a Home Lending Specialist to confirm how this may impact your application.
