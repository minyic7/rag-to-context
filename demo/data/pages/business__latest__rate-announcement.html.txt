## Understanding rate changes
Why are interest rates changing? When the Reserve Bank Board decides that a change in monetary policy should occur, it specifies a new target for the cash rate. A decision to ease policy is reflected in a new lower target for the cash rate, while a decision to tighten policy is reflected in a higher target. View the latest statements by the Reserve Bank on monetary policy decisions.
