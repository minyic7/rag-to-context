## Securely create your profile in under 10 minutes
You’ll need to: Download the latest version of the CommBank app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store Open the CommBank app and choose ‘Sign up to CommBank’ Select your CommBank product, provide personal details and complete an ID check using a physical ID Continue with your product application How to download the CommBank app: If you’re on a computer, scan the QR code using your smartphone camera to open this page Once you’re on this page, tap the buttons near the QR code to download the latest version of the CommBank app on the Apple App Store or Google Play store

## You’ll need to:
Download the latest version of the CommBank app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store
Open the CommBank app and choose ‘Sign up to CommBank’
Select your CommBank product, provide personal details and complete an ID check using a physical ID
Continue with your product application
How to download the CommBank app: If you’re on a computer, scan the QR code using your smartphone camera to open this page Once you’re on this page, tap the buttons near the QR code to download the latest version of the CommBank app on the Apple App Store or Google Play store

## How to download the CommBank app:
If you’re on a computer, scan the QR code using your smartphone camera to open this page
Once you’re on this page, tap the buttons near the QR code to download the latest version of the CommBank app on the Apple App Store or Google Play store

## Things you should know
Here's more information on our security capabilities and simple steps you can take to protect yourself every day, wherever you are online. QR codes are provided for your convenience and the location you are taken to will not ask you sensitive information, banking information like your NetBank Client ID, password, or NetCode; or include a link to login. Always type commbank.com.au into a browser or use the CommBank app to securely access your banking.  For more information on protecting yourself online, visit commbank.com.au/hoax . The CommBank app is free to download however your mobile network provider charges you for accessing data on your phone. The CommBank app is available on Android and iOS operating systems (minimum operating system version requirements may apply). Learn more at commbank.com.au/app . Terms and conditions are available on the CommBank app. NetBank access with NetCode SMS is required. Apple and the Apple logo are trademarks of Apple Inc., registered in the U.S. and other countries. App Store is a service mark of Apple Inc. Android and Google Play are trademarks of Google Inc.
