## Frequently asked questions

## What is fleet car management?
Fleet car management is the ongoing management of a group of vehicles owned or leased by a business. This can include selecting, purchasing or leasing vehicles, as well as maintenance and repair. Fleet car management can improve efficiency, aim to reduce costs and ensure vehicles are safe to operate.

## When should I choose a novated lease or fleet lease?
A novated lease is a salary packaging contract in which your employer helps you lease a car by taking payments from your pre-tax salary. You could benefit from tax savings and the convenience of having payments handled by your employer.
A fleet lease is an arrangement where a sole trader or business leases multiple vehicles. Rates may be more competitive for those leasing a large group of vehicles, however there might be higher upfront costs.
It's always best to consider your needs and financial situation before making a choice.

## What is SG Fleet?
SG Fleet is a fleet car management provider which offers vehicle leasing, salary packaging and mobility services. It works with the public sector and private businesses of all sizes across Australia, New Zealand and the UK.
