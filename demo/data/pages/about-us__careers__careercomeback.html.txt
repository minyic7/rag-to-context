## About the Program
Our Career Comeback Program is a dedicated initiative designed to support professionals returning to the workforce after a career break of two years or more. Whether your time away was for family, study, travel, health, or other personal reasons, this program offers a structured and supportive pathway to reignite your career, refresh your skills, and rebuild confidence. We run multiple intakes each year, with applications open in March, June, and October. Roles are available across a wide range of business areas, including (but not limited to): Banking, Finance, and Accounting Project Management and Operations Risk and Compliance Customer Service Most opportunities are based in Sydney, with additional roles available interstate from time to time depending on business needs. ## What's involved?
A structured 12-week program designed to provide you with the re-entry support and training you need to thrive
Flexible work options, hours and arrangements that work for you*
Comprehensive on-boarding and industry-specific skills training
A structured professional development program to help you re-build your confidence and show up as your best self
A designated career coach and a dedicated buddy
A people leader and program manager who will assist you in your transition back to work, with regular check-ins
* Flexible working arrangements depend on the nature of the role. We’ll work with you on an arrangement that best suits you.

## Eligibility criteria
Our Career Comeback Program is designed for people who: Have taken a career break of two years or more (essential) Are motivated to return to work on a permanent basis (both part-time and full-time options available) Have at least three years of relevant professional experience Possess a growth mindset and strong motivation to succeed Have permanent Australian working rights

## Application process
Step 1: Online application Submit your CV and a cover letter outlining your career history and motivation to join the Career Comeback Program. Step 2: Telephone discussion If you have relevant experience and fit the program requirements, we’ll call you to have a chat about your application and invite you to interview with us. At this point we may also ask you to complete some psychometric testing so that we can assess your active learning and problem-solving skills. Step 3: First interview Your first interview will be with a number of business representatives from the team that you’ve applied for and will involve a variety of behavioural and technical questions.  We’ll let you know if your interview will be held virtually or in person, and our Career Comeback Program Manager will help prepare you for your interview and share some helpful tips and tricks before you meet with us. If you're successful in progressing through this stage, you will be invited to a second interview in the following weeks. Step 4: Second interview Your second interview will be held by a more senior member of the team and most likely will be in person so that you can get a sense of our offices and team culture. Step 5: Offer Successful candidates will receive news of their offer at least six weeks prior to the program start date.

## I don’t meet all the eligibility requirements, can I still apply?
To be considered for the Career Comeback Program, candidates must meet the below criteria:
You are currently on a career break (for two years or more)*
You have at least 3+ years of relevant professional experience
* Taking part in consulting/freelance work or having generated some income from non-professional activities (including work in Academia) does not disqualify applicants.

## I’ve never worked in banking before. Does that matter?
No. We have a wide range of opportunities available that welcome a variety of backgrounds. For example, in our non-client facing teams, candidates with backgrounds in operations, technology, risk, consulting, project management or business law are encouraged to apply.

## Once I submit my application, what are the next steps?
You'll receive an update on your application within 5 working days. If you have the experience and passion we’re looking for, we’ll give you a call to chat further.

## What level of positions and salaries are offered through the Career Comeback Program?
We recruit candidates with varying levels of experience and have hired candidates with 3-20 years' experience across Senior Associate, Manager and Senior Manager positions. The salaries on offer vary, and depend on the specific role and your level of experience.

## What is the start date?
We recruit for Career Comeback multiple times throughout the year and look for candidates across a variety of areas including Banking, Technology and Operations. We typically allow for at least six weeks between offer and start date to allow for candidates ample time to prepare for work. For more information on our upcoming program dates, you can contact us at [email protected] .

## What are the hours?
At CommBank, we work flexibly. For some, this may mean four days per week, or finishing at 4pm each day to pick up your kids. Flexible working arrangements depend on the nature of the role and team you apply for, and we’re happy to work with you to understand how this opportunity can flex to suit your lifestyle.

## What locations are available?
We have Career Comeback opportunities available across Australia. Specific locations for each intake may vary depending on business needs and available roles. To view current opportunities and locations, please click ‘Apply now’.
