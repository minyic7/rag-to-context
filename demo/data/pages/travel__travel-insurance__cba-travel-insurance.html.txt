## Any existing medical conditions?
Why this is important Let’s debunk a myth - being honest about a medical condition doesn’t always mean you can’t get travel insurance. Look through the inclusions lists in the PDS to understand what’s automatically covered and give Cover-More a call if you’re not sure. What’s listed? Conditions including acne, asthma, epilepsy, high blood pressure and more may be covered under certain conditions. While others, including these, are automatically covered (subject to meeting the PDS criteria): Bell’s Palsy Coeliac disease Gastric & peptic ulcers Graves’ disease Hiatus hernia Raynaud’s disease and more For a full list of medical conditions and eligibility criteria, check our PDS . Mine’s not there. Now what? If your existing medical conditions or your travel companion’s condition(s) doesn’t meet the criteria for automatic cover, you’ll need to complete an online health assessment as part of purchasing your travel insurance or call 1300 467 951 for help. You’ll instantly know if they’re covered. If approved, an additional premium may be required to include cover for your existing medical condition.

## Cooling-off period
You can cancel or change your policy at any time before you leave home. If you cancel a policy within the cooling-off period (within 21 days of the date of purchase) Cover-More will give you your money back, provided you have not already made a claim under the policy or departed on your journey. Call Cover-More on 1300 467 951 (8am-5pm Mon - Fri and 9am-4pm Sat, Sydney/Melbourne time) within the cooling-off period, to cancel your policy.
