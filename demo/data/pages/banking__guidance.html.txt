## The CommBank app can help you stay in control of your money
Spend Tracker : Categorises your debit and credit card transactions (e.g. eating out, utilities, groceries) so you can see exactly what, where and how much you’re spending each month. Tell me more
Transaction Notifications : Receive notifications when you pay or are charged for something using your debit or credit card. You can create rules around what types of transactions you’re notified about and how often you’re notified. Tell me more
Bill Sense : Predicts when you have an upcoming bill due before it arrives, so you can plan ahead. Tell me more
Cash Flow View gives you a better picture of your income, spending and saving habits across your CommBank accounts. And shows how you’re trending month-to-month. Tell me more
Goal Tracker: Whether you have a savings goal in mind or want to build a buffer for those ‘just in case’ moments, Goal Tracker 1 helps you set and reach your goals. Tell me more
