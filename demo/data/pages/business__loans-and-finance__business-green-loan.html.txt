## What is green lending?
Green lending refers to loans or financing provided for environmentally friendly projects or initiatives, such as renewable energy projects (e.g. solar, wind, hydro), energy-efficient buildings or fit-outs, sustainable agriculture, electronic vehicles or waste reduction and recycling initiatives. If the initiatives meet certain environmental or sustainability criteria, borrowers may be offered more competitive lending terms, such as lower interest rates.

## How do I qualify?
To qualify for our Business Green Loan, you must use your loan for an eligible asset or project such as 1 : Renewable energy e.g. solar, wind, hydropower and batteries Clean transport e.g. electric vehicles and charging Energy efficient assets e.g. highly efficient motors, lighting, heating and cooling Green building upgrades e.g. low e-glazing, insulation and air source heat pumps Circular economy adapted products e.g. recycling, refurbishment and composting Environmentally sustainable management of living natural resources and land use Sustainable water and wastewater management including water efficiency and groundwater pollution prevention We may approve other assets or projects depending on your business. Speak to your Business Banker or request a callback to discuss any proposals.

## Frequently asked questions

## What's a Business Green Loan?
Business Green Loan helps finance projects that work towards lowering emissions and protecting the environment. Funds from the loan must be used towards an eligible asset or project.

## Why should I apply for a Business Green Loan?
Implementing sustainable practices may help you:
Reduce business costs
Improve business resilience
Address evolving stakeholder expectations
Expand into new markets
Our Business Green Loan is designed to make it easier for you to do this.

## What do I need to provide when applying for a Business Green Loan?
In addition to the standard business credit requirements you’ll need documents detailing your eligible asset or project and proof of how you’ll use the funds (e.g. quotes, project proposals and budgets, before and after photos of a site or detailed evidence of your eligible asset or project) 2 .
Your Business Banker will confirm which documents you’ll need.
