## Determining the Winners
The random electronic prize draw will take place at 11:00am (AEDT) on 4th March 2026 at Trade Promotions and Lotteries Pty Ltd (ACN 601 297 330), Level 2, 11 York St, Sydney NSW 2000 (Draw). The first 18 entries from Eligible Entrants drawn in New South Wales, the first 9 entries from Eligible Entrants drawn in Western Australia and the first 5 entries from Eligible Entrants drawn in Queensland, as determined by the Promoter, will be awarded a Prize (Winners). The 9 Winners of Prizes for the Sydney Semi Final will be drawn first, with a further 9 Winners of Prizes for the Sydney Final drawn after.
At the time of the Draw, the Promoter will randomly draw a further 15 additional entries in each of New South Wales, Western Australia and Queensland from Eligible Entrants to be reserve winners if a Prize is unclaimed or cannot be delivered to or accepted by a Winner in accordance with these Terms and Conditions (Reserve Winners).
Each Eligible Entrant is only eligible to receive one Prize.
