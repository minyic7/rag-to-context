## Sustainable finance & ESG
Our Sustainable finance and ESG team supports clients in taking actions that drive better environmental, social (ESG) outcomes, by linking their sustainability strategy to their financing arrangements. Key capabilities include structuring and facilitating: Use of Proceeds Instruments – the proceeds of capital are used to finance new or existing projects or activities display green and/or social characteristics. For example, a Green Loan, Social Bond or Sustainability Loan. Sustainability-Linked Instruments – proceeds from financing are available for general corporate purposes, with the cost of funding tied to agreed targets that drive improved environmental or social outcomes. For example a Sustainability-Linked Bond, Loan or Derivative.

Our carbon markets team enables financing and risk management solutions across global carbon markets that support our clients to implement their climate strategies. ## Green & sustainable trade
We are proud to be a leader in supporting underlying trade transactions that facilitate positive environmental, social or governance impacts. Financing structures and types include: Green trade finance – trade financing that supports activities that lead to improved sustainability outcomes, with solutions including: Guarantees Letters of credit Stand-by letters of credit Trade finance loans Sustainability-linked trade finance – we provide working capital and documentary trade solutions (import/export) that offer financial incentives triggered by meeting jointly agreed sustainability performance targets. These facilities help our clients align their financial arrangements with their sustainability strategies and to take actions that drive improved environmental, social (ESG) outcomes.
