## About CommBiz International Payments & Foreign Exchange
For most cross-currency International Payments, we will pay any correspondent bank fees for you. This does not include payments in Japanese Yen. Refer to the CommBiz Terms and Conditions for more details.
CommBiz International Payments & Foreign Exchange (IPFX) is an online platform.
Undertake foreign exchange transactions including creating spot or forward exchange contracts, make domestic and international payments in foreign currencies, using your AUD or foreign currency accounts

## Enter payment details
On the dashboard, click Make a payment and then the link Enter payment detail
If you're choosing an existing beneficiary first select the Payment Currency from the drop down. Otherwise you can create a new beneficiary on the go
You have the option to fund from an existing same currency account (if you have one setup with CommBank and linked to CommBiz)
Alternatively, select the Debit currency from the dropdown menu
Add the amount you want to pay by clicking on the button beside Payment Amount or Debit Amount. Payment Amount is the amount paid in the currency you want to send; Debit amount represents the amount that will be deducted from your account
Add the reference for your beneficiary ,  for example, their invoice number
Choose the purpose of the payment from dropdown
Click Add or if you want to setup multiple payments, choose Add & Enter Next Payment and repeat the above steps

## Note on adding multiple payments and currency cut-off times
The next available date is already calculated based on the currency cut-off and public holidays in both Australia and the beneficiary currency
If you have setup multiple payments in different currencies and the cut-off time has passed for one of those currencies, the rest of your payments will not occur until the next available date
Setup individual payments if you wish to process payments before currency cut-off time

## Select how you want to pay
Choose from the first available date
Or toggle over to schedule paymen t and select a future date from the calendar
If you choose a future date, you will have to schedule using a new or existing forward exchange contract
Forward exchange products have risks associated with them. Please refer to CommBank’s Foreign Exchange PDS for further information or call 1300 222 339

## Fund from an existing or new FX trade
Click Apply FX Rate button against the trade you want to utilise for the payment
You will notice under Action that you can pre-deliver an existing FX contract to fund the payment
Alternatively, you can select the button Create New FX Trade on this page to source the current exchange rate for the transaction
Whether you’re pre-delivering, extending or creating a new trade, a pop-up box will appear where you can action your trade depending on your transaction requirements

## Authorise your trades
There are several ways to Authorise your trades
Pending authorisations are displayed after you submit your payment (as per the previous steps above)
Or you can find Authorisations section on your dashboard
Select the checkboxes next to each trade you want to action
Add your CommBiz security credentials to authorise or reject the payments

## Create FX Trade
Click Create FX Trade on your dashboard
Setup currencies using the Client Buys and Client Sells dropdown options
Add the amount in the field beside the currency you want to Buy or Sell
Update the Value Date (if applicable) and add Trade Notes (for your reference details)
View the FX rate by clicking Request Quote . The rate will refresh every 6 seconds and time out after 30 seconds, select Request Rate again if it times out
Click Accept & Execute when you are ready to proceed with the quoted rate. The currency conversion may go for dealer intervention. When this happens, please wait for the next instructions on the screen
You will receive a notification after its successfully executed. Please note: once accepted all FX trades are to be fully settled and authorised by currency cut-off time on the value date. If trades remain unsettled, CommBank can choose to close out the trade. Please refer to the Foreign Exchange PDS for further information or call 1300 222 339

## Outstanding Trades
Select Outstanding FX Trades on your dashboard
By clicking on the arrow icon next to each trade, further information will appear
Pre-delivery or extend trades
Attach settlement instructions
View the deal history
Update Trade Notes by clicking the pencil icon
Download trade information
View how to action each of these by following the steps under the headings below

## Pre-deliver trades
Select the arrow icon next to the trade, click Pre-deliver
Add the pre-delivery amount in the Buy or Sell field
Update the Value Date
Click Request Quote .  The rate will refresh every 6 seconds and time out after 30 seconds, select Request Rate again if it times out
Click Accept & Execute when you are ready to proceed.  The currency conversion may go for dealer intervention. Please wait for next instructions on the screen

## Extend trades
Select the arrow icon next to the trade, click Extend
Add the amount your want to extend in the Buy or Sell field
Update the Value Date
Click Request Quote. The rate will refresh every 6 seconds and time out after 30 seconds, select Request Rate again if it times out
Click Accept & Execute when you are ready to proceed.  The currency conversion may go for dealer intervention. Please wait for the next instructions on the screen

## Attach Settlement Instructions
Select the white checkbox next to the Trade. You can select multiple trades in one go. If the checkbox is grey, select the arrow and find Remove Settlement Instructions button to update and attach new instructions
Scroll down to Trade Summary at the bottom of the screen
Click Attach Settlement Instructions
Choose your Settlement method from the dropdown
Add the amount and type a reference for your records

## View Trade History
Select the arrow next to the Trade
Click Trade History .  View deal history of the trade including when it has been pre-delivered or extended

## View Status History
Select the arrow next to the Trade
Click Status History . Status History shows the processing of the trade, like who it has been authorised by or if the trade has been fully processed, cancelled or declined

## Customise your indicative rates
The indicative spot rates on your dashboard are customisable meaning you can edit and prioritise the view of your preferred currency combinations
Click Edit currency list on your dashboard
Choose your preferred currency combinations from the dropdown options
Click Save . Once saved these same currency combinations will continue to display as your chosen indicative spot rates each time you enter the IPFX Dashboard
