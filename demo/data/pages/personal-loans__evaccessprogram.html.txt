## What is the EV Access Program (EVAP)?
Eligible customers can get a special rate on a Secured Fixed Rate Car Loan, which includes an energy-efficient discount, plus unique discounts on selected electric vehicles when purchasing an eligible electric or plug-in hybrid vehicle.
Eligible customers can get a special rate on a Secured Fixed Rate Car Loan, which includes an energy-efficient discount, plus unique discounts on selected electric vehicles when purchasing an eligible electric or plug-in hybrid vehicle.

## Who is eligible?
Eligible Essential Workers Individuals employed as teachers, instructors, medical professionals, veterinarians, government officials (ambulance, fire fighters, judges/magistrates/mediators, parliamentarians/councillors, police), or care workers OR Individuals with a gross income of $100,000 p.a. or less.

## Eligible Essential Workers
Individuals employed as teachers, instructors, medical professionals, veterinarians, government officials (ambulance, fire fighters, judges/magistrates/mediators, parliamentarians/councillors, police), or care workers

## ORIndividuals with a gross income of $100,000 p.a. or less.

## Which vehicles are eligible?
Any electric or plug-in hybrid vehicle emitting less than 75g of CO2 per kilometre, with a vehicle price and loan amount less than or equal to $55,000.
For example, you could get a BYD Dolphin Essential from $29,990 plus on-road costs through Cars for CommBank platform.
