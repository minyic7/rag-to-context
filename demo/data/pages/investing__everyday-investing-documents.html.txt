## Statements
To find your statements in the CommBank app:​ 1. Navigate to your account ​ Open the CommBank app on your mobile device and go to ‘Accounts’ at the bottom of the screen to find your Everyday Investing account.​
2. Tap on ‘Manage' In your Everyday Investing account, tap 'Manage’ at the top of the screen.​
3. Tap on ‘View Statement History' ​ Under ‘Statements and more’, tap ‘View Statement History’ to view your statements. ​
