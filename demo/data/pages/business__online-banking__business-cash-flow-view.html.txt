## How to access your business cash flow
If you're a CommBank customer Get a clearer view of your business cash flow in just a few steps:
Download or open the CommBank app
Select Accounts at the bottom of your screen
Choose an eligible business account . 2 Eligible business accounts will automatically appear in your business cash flow view
Select Go to your Business Cash Flow
You'll see an overview of your income, expenses and trends to help manage your cash flow effectively.
Open a CommBank business account online - it only takes a few minutes
Download the CommBank app to view and manage your business cash flow anytime, anywhere.
