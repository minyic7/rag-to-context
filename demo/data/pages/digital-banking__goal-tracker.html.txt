Title: Set and track a savings goal – Goal Tracker – CommBank

## Finding it hard to save regularly? We make it easier.
Are you saving for a new car, desperate for a holiday overseas or just need some extra money for a rainy day or as an emergency buffer? Set a savings goal using Goal Tracker and we’ll break it down into weekly targets - so it’s less overwhelming or completely out of reach. We’ll also help you set up automatic payments to your goal so you never miss your weekly targets and can top up your goal when it suits you. If you go off track, we’ll show you how to top up your goal or adjust it.

## Get a handle on your spending. It’ll help you save.
Your spending decisions, no matter how small, all add up.
Knowing exactly what you’re spending (and if you’re overspending) can help you work out how much you can comfortably save.
Transaction Notifications together with Spend Tracker and Cash Flow View in the CommBank app, give you info to help you make better decisions about your finances, setting you up for future success.
Turn Transaction Notifications on to see, check and track your debit and credit card spending. Then Spend Tracker will automatically categorise your spending so you can see where you’re spending the most – it can be an eye-opener.
