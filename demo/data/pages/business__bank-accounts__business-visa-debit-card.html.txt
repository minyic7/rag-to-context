## Frequently asked questions

## Does my Business Visa Debit Card have controls and limits?
You can use controls on your card to manage your spending. Go to Card settings in the CommBank app to set up controls.
Business Visa Debit Cards don't have a transaction limit, however the daily withdrawal limit is $2,000. You can change your withdrawal limit in the CommBank app or NetBank. Withdrawal limits are calculated on a 24-hour period, midnight to midnight (Syd/Melb time).

## Can I have more than one Business Visa Debit Card?
Each signatory on a business account may only have one Business Visa Debit Card. However, you can add additional eligible signatories to an account who can each order a Business Visa Debit Card. Find out how to give someone access to your business account .

## Does the Business Visa Debit Card earn points?
The Business Visa Debit Card doesn't earn any points.

## How do I notify CommBank before I travel overseas?
Notify us in NetBank before you travel overseas so we can keep an eye out for suspicious activity on your account while you're away.

## Why am I having trouble ordering a card online?
Your business account may be ineligible for a card or have restrictions on it. For the fastest way to find out why, message us in the CommBank app. Otherwise call us on 13 1998 , or visit your nearest branch.

## How do I activate my Business Visa Debit Card?
Follow these instructions to activate your card in NetBank or the CommBank app.

## How do I lock my Business Visa Debit Card?
You can lock your card in the CommBank app or in NetBank.
Locking your card won't prevent recurring payments and Apple Pay, Google Pay or Samsung Pay transactions from being processed.
If you've locked your card because you've found a suspicious transaction, message us in the CommBank app and start the chat with "dispute transaction". Or you can call us any time on 13 1998.
