Title: Qkr for schools – a mobile payments app

## What is Qkr?
Qkr is a mobile app designed to accept and streamline payments for schools. Onboard parents, P&Cs and suppliers to the app to securely receive and reconcile payments for your tuckshop, uniform shop, excursions and more.

## Frequently asked questions

## What are the rates and fees?
Custom pricing and fees are available. Contact your Relationship Manager or our Merchant Payment Solutions on 1800 730 554 for a quote and more pricing information.

## I'm a parent, how do I get my school on board?
Advise your school of CommBank's tailored school banking solutions, and encourage them to reach out to our Merchant Payment Solutions on 1800 730 554 .

## I'm a parent, and my school uses Qkr, how do I start using it?
Simply download the Qkr app from the App store or Google Play. Once your school sends you the relevant login details, register your child’s details on the app and then you are ready to order and pay for meals and other items.
