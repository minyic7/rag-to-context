Find options for discharging, selling, refinancing, substituting a security (property or cash), releasing a guarantor or repaying your loan.

## Discharge your sold property

## Need to keep your information private?
If you prefer not to share your personal details with co-borrowers during the discharge process, you’re welcome to submit a separate discharge authority form.  

For further assistance, contact us on 1300 219 166 (or +612 4749 0774 if you're overseas).
