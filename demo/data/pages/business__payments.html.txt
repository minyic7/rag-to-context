## Frequently asked questions

## What are merchant services and how do they work?
Merchant services are designed to help your business accept payments, whether in-store, online, or on the go. These services include a range of payment solutions such as EFTPOS terminals , eCommerce payment gateways with value-added features such as surcharging and refunds.

## What's a merchant facility?
A merchant facility is the arrangement or service provided by a bank or payment provider that enables a business (the merchant) to accept electronic payments from customers. This includes credit cards, debit cards, and other payment methods. It usually involves:
Hardware: EFTPOS terminals or integrated POS system
Software: Payment gateways for online transactions
Merchant account: A special type of account where funds from card transactions are held before being transferred to the business’ bank account

## What are the best ways to accept payments as a small business?
If you need to take payments in person or in store, consider an EFTPOS terminal . If you’re taking payments using a website, consider a payment gateway solution .

## What payment solution is best for the retail industry?
If you're taking payments in store, you’ll need an EFTPOS terminal to accept card and digital wallet payments. If your retail business operates entirely online, you’ll need a payment gateway solution to securely accept online orders and payments. Explore our retail payment solutions .

## How do I apply for a CommBank merchant terminal?
Applying for a merchant terminal online is quick and easy, typically taking 15 minutes. So your business can start accepting customer payments as quickly as possible, we’ll need the following details:
Your business registration with ASIC
Your Australian Business Number (ABN), business structure and registered address
Details of every director, partner or trustee depending on your business structure
You can apply online in 15 minutes or call 1800 730 544 to speak to a merchant specialist.
Once your application is approved, we’ll send you information about your terminal and how to get started.
