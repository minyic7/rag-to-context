Title: Rewards & benefits - CommBank

## Balance that inspires
Empower your wellbeing and thrive in every moment.
Looking to shape your health and wellbeing? Our Wellbeing program offers a range of benefits that include comprehensive and affordable health insurance, engaging fitness programs, and wellbeing support. Whether it's about staying physically fit, mentally resilient, or helping to ensure your overall good health, we have the resources that can support you. When your health and wellbeing is not going to plan, we also have a range of services available to support you.

## CBHS Health Fund
Created just for the CommBank community, CBHS offers exclusive health cover for our employees and their families. Choose from a range of affordable options, based on your health needs and budget – think peace of mind for unexpected hospital trips, savings on dental and optical, and much more! Plus, your cover comes with the genuine care and value only a not-for-profit, member-owned health fund can deliver.

## Fitness Passport
Imagine if you and your family could access an unparalleled range of fitness facilities, including gyms and pools without the cost or hassle of multiple memberships. Fitness Passport offers a variety of cost-efficient membership options allowing you to access fitness facilities at home, near work and even whilst you are on holidays.

## Thrive including telehealth
Thrive is our holistic wellbeing platform, available 24/7 globally to help you feel your best all year round. You can access hundreds of free events, resources, and tools across four core pillars of life, mind, body, and finance. Whether you're navigating a life change, managing stress, or planning for retirement, Thrive is here to support your mental and physical health, build financial confidence and make it easier to focus on what matters most. Thrive also includes access to GP care onsite in certain locations or via telehealth nationwide to support your overall health and wellbeing, both bulk billed.

## Not feeling your best self?
For when life does not go to plan, we offer free, confidential support through our Employee Assistance Program, including short-term coaching and counselling for you and your family. We also offer services like second medical opinions and counselling during serious health challenges as well as free income protection.

## Shape your financial future
Build confidence and security for what’s next.
Ready to shape your financial wellbeing? Explore our Financial benefits and unlock exclusive banking perks. We offer salary sacrifice options, expert financial coaching and access to our super plan with Australian Retirement Trust. Whether you're saving for what’s next, planning for retirement or just want to feel more confident with your money, we’re here to help you make informed decisions that can build you a brighter financial future.

## Employee banking benefits
As a CommBank employee, you get access to banking perks beyond the everyday. We’re talking better rates and personalised support to help you buy a home, manage expenses or just plan for what’s next with real confidence.

## Employee share offers
CommBank offers employee share plans to recognise and reward employees, including the employee share acquisition plan (an award of up to $1,000) and employee salary sacrifice share plan, that allows you to salary sacrifice towards purchasing shares.

## Staff deals
Get exclusive discounts from brands you know and love. With our staff deals platform you’ll save on everything from tech and travel to homewares and more, all for your personal use!

## Salary sacrifice
Boost your financial wellbeing with our range of salary sacrifice schemes. These include extra super contributions, novated leases and subscription car services, share plans and workplace giving.

## Grow without limits
Unlock opportunities to learn, lead, and innovate.
Our Career and Learning benefits are designed to fuel your growth, support your development goals, and help you shape what’s next in your career. Whether you're building new capabilities, exploring study support, or expanding your expertise, we’re here to empower your learning journey and ensure you feel supported every step of the way.

## Learning resources
Unlock your potential with access to our learning platform. You’ll discover new ways to learn and grow your future-ready skills.

## AI Every Day
Through our AI Every Day program, employees gain the confidence and skills to feel prepared for the future of work using AI safely and responsibly, making their work feel easier, more achievable and more energising.

## Grow with us
Our teams have opportunities to develop their skills, work with a community of experts and make a real impact on the future. You’ll be part of a collaborative, supportive team where you can develop new skills, adapt and stay ahead in a world that’s always changing.

## Time that matters
Make space for what truly counts - you and your loved ones.
Need time to recharge, care or give back? We’ve got leave options for real life. Whether you're welcoming a new child, navigating a personal moment, or volunteering in your community, we’ve got you. Includes parental leave, life leave and volunteering leave. If eligible, you can even purchase up to 4 extra weeks leave with our salary sacrifice option.

## Life leave
We all need regular breaks from work to rest, recover and reset. With life leave you receive up to a week of additional paid leave each work anniversary, so long as you meet the eligibility criteria. Use it however you like.

## Parental leave
Welcoming a child into the world is exciting but quite daunting! So, whether it's your first, or one of many, we know that it's a life-changing experience. We want you to be able to take time out from your job and enjoy this special time. As part of our Parental Leave program, we also pay your super contributions for up to 34 weeks of unpaid parental leave.

## Pet leave
If your pet needs emergency veterinary care or if it sadly passes away, our Pet Leave policy allows you to take up to 2 days per anniversary year.

## Paid volunteering leave
CommBank offers most employees volunteer leave, so they can support causes that matter to them. Whether it’s helping those in need, following a passion, or strengthening community ties, volunteering is one of the most rewarding ways we create brighter futures together.

## Lifestyle that elevates
Enjoy experiences and benefits designed for you.
Make life that little bit easier at work and home. Our Lifestyle extras support your everyday needs. Maybe it’s flexible working options, trusted childcare services, or even pet insurance to help protect your furry family members. Whether you're juggling work and home, or just looking for more balance, these benefits give you peace of mind and more time back for what matters.

## Flexible ways of working
We have a long history of supporting flexible ways of working to help our people thrive and our commitment continues in a way that balances the needs of our customers, teams and our people. Everyone has the opportunity to request a formal flexible working arrangement for their individual circumstances, use flex enabling leave, and the majority of our roles support location flex, and occasional flex.

## Flexibility with your next car
Looking for flexibility with your next car? We have options if you’re looking for a novated lease, or for an EV subscription service. Both are available through our salary sacrifice programs.

## Income protection
Life doesn’t always go to plan. If you’re unable to work due to illness or injury, our free income protection policy offers financial support for up to two years so you can focus on your recovery.

## Workplace giving
For more than a century, our people have been helping to build brighter futures for Australians in need. Through the CommBank Staff Foundation, we make it easy for our people to give back. Every dollar donated through workplace giving is matched by CommBank, doubling the impact and helping to create lasting change in communities right across Australia.

## Things you should know
All employee benefits are subject to change (including withdrawal) by the Group at any time at its complete discretion. Employees are responsible for reviewing and understanding the eligibility criteria and full terms and conditions for each benefit before accessing or utilising the benefit. None of the above information should be construed as providing financial or tax advice to employees. If you are thinking of taking advantage of any of these benefits, you should consider whether you should obtain your own independent tax and/or financial advice.
