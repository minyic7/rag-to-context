Title: Natural disasters support - CommBank

## Affected by significant weather?
CommBank's Emergency Assistance is available to customers and businesses affected by bushfires, floods, storms and other disasters. We can provide customised financial assistance such as loan restructuring and waiving of selected fees and charges.

## Prepare

## Get in the know
Understand the types of natural disasters that might affect your area
Save the Bureau of Meteorology (BOM) site and download your State’s Emergency Alert app
Know how to identify and manage your stress
Identify your key contacts
Complete the Community Connector Card so you know where to get information in an emergency and grow your community

## Get organised
Complete the Readiness Wheel

## Make a plan
Identify evacuation routes and safe locations
Prepare an emergency kit with essential supplies

## Respond

## Immediate actions:
Follow official instructions and evacuation orders
Prioritise the safety of yourself and your loved ones
Keep up to date with your State’s emergency warnings and alerts
CommBank customer support:
Financial Support Hub

## Recover

## Assessing damage
Document any damage to your property and belongings – take photos where you can
Contact your insurance provider to start claims processes. If you have insurance with Hollard, you can make a claim online

## Insurance
Home insurance offers protection against loss or damage to your building and contents caused by natural disasters – but there are some things it doesn’t cover . Review your policy to understand whether your cover still suits your insurance needs. This Moneysmart guide can help understand storm, flood and fire cover. Keeping your home and appliances in good condition and well maintained can help prevent damage and unexpected costs. Be prepared with these home maintenance tips . Review the below checklists to best prepare your: Car and home for a bushfire Business for a bushfire Home and car for flooding Home and car for a cyclone Home for a storm
