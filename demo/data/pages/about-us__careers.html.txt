## Life Leave
We all need to take regular breaks from work to rest, recover and reset. In recognition of this, the Group offers up to 38 hours of additional paid life leave each year to team members who meet the eligibility criteria.

## Fitness Passport
With Fitness Passport, you and your family can access an unparalleled range of fitness facilities, including gyms and pools, at a discounted price, without the cost or hassle of juggling multiple memberships. If you are a CBHS member, you may be entitled to a further discount!

## Parental Leave
Our inclusive parental support options are designed to help all working parents manage their caring and family responsibilities. Permanent employees can access up to 18 weeks’ paid parental leave that can be taken flexibly depending on your caring responsibility.

## Thrive GP onsite and Telehealth Services
Finding time and space to look after your health isn't always easy. To help you prioritise your health and wellbeing during the work day, we offer you easy and direct access to healthcare, without any out-of-pocket costs for your consultation.
