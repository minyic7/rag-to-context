5.84 % PA Interest rate *

## Why choose Simple Home Loan
Enjoy a competitive variable rate with the flexibility to negotiate your rate when you apply
Link up to two Everyday Offset accounts to save on interest 1
Make unlimited additional repayments , redraw 2 funds, or choose to make Interest Only payments 3
Top up your loan by $10,000 or more to access extra funds when needed
Receive up to $15 cashback each month if you're eligible for CommBank Yello Benefits ⁴ . We’ll waive the requirement to make 15 transactions, if in the prior month you settle an eligible home loan with us and you hold an eligible transaction account. It’s a simple way to help you ease into the program and start enjoying cashback benefits. Learn more here
Save $300 with a waived establishment fee when applying online, with a CommBank lender or your Broker ^^

## Rates & fees
$300 upfront establishment fee
$0 upfront establishment fee -limited time offer for First Home Buyers ^^ .
$20 monthly loan service fee (you may be eligible for up to $15 monthly cashback if you meet CommBank Yello eligibility 4 )
$10 monthly Offset Feature fee, if one or more Everyday Offset account is linked
Other fees and charges may apply
