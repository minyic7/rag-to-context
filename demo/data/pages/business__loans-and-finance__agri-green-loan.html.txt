## Understanding agri finance
Agri finance, or agricultural finance, refers to specialised financial services and products for the agricultural sector, including farmers. Our Agri Green Loan is one of the many ways we can support our Agribusiness customers.

Improving management of waste

## Frequently Asked Questions

## Is there an Agri Green Loan limit?
The Agri Green Loan has a minimum of $10,000 and there is no maximum subject to credit approval.

## What do I need to provide when applying for the Agri Green Loan?
In addition to the standard business credit requirements to qualify you will be required to provide documentation to demonstrate the eligibility of the Agri Green Loan purpose, and verification of the use of funds post funding. Sources of evidence could include but are not limited to invoices, project proposals and budgets, before and after photos of a site or detailed evidence of your accredited environmental project.

## What should I do if my project requires an upfront deposit?
If an upfront deposit is required, you’ll need to pay this from your own savings.
We’ll reimburse (pay) the deposit for your Agri Green Loan once we receive the final signed invoice subject to credit approval providing the deposit was completed in the last 12 months and that the loan meets our Agri Green Loan criteria.

## What can I use the Agri Green Loan for?
Some examples of eligible environmental projects include:
Soil sequestration and development of carbon sinks
Converting degraded land to improve soil quality
Planting trees or installing shelter belts
Improving management of waste
Switching to regenerative farming practices
Deploying precision use of chemicals
Shifting land use from monoculture to polyculture
Promoting biodiversity and healthy ecosystems
Water efficiency upgrades
Preventing, controlling or restoring erosion
Installing renewable energy
Reducing greenhouse gas emissions
Speak to your Agribusiness Executive or request a callback to discuss other eligible projects.
Note: You can't use the Agri Green Loan to buy a property. This loan must be used to invest in equipment or practices that help reduce the impact on the environment.
