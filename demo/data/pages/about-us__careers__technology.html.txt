## Meet Blair Hudson - Chief Engineer, GenAI at CommBank
Blair joined CommBank driven by the opportunity to make a real difference for Australia. As a chief engineer in GenAI, Blair leads the team at a technical level, ensuring ambitious goals are met and innovation thrives.
What sets CommBank apart? For Blair, it’s the belief that anything is achievable. The bank’s vision for GenAI is bold, and every project has the potential to create meaningful impact, even for our most vulnerable customers.

## Our locations
We support our people with the flexibility to balance where work is done with at least half their time each month connecting in the office. We also have many other flexible working options available including changing start and finish times, part-time arrangements and job share to name a few. The opening of our tech hubs across the country means you no longer need to be near our Sydney head office and can be based near Melbourne, Adelaide, or Perth.
