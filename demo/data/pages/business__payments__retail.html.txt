## Frequently asked questions

## What are the key features to look for in retail payment solutions?
Important features for retail businesses include settlement time, i.e. how quickly funds arrive in your account; supported payment methods (card, digital wallets, contactless, etc.); integration with your POS system; a simple fee structure; and security and compliance, including fraud protection.

## What are retail payment solutions?
Retail payment solutions allow a business to accept payments in a variety of ways (in store, at a pop-up event, or online) that meet customer expectations. This might include hardware, such as a terminal or card reader, or eCommerce products , like an online payment gateway that connects to your website shopping cart.
