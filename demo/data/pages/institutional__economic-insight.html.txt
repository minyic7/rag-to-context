## Our data and analysis
The Global Economic & Markets Research (GEMR) team provides clients with regular, real-time research and insights enriched by CommBank internally generated data, showing changes in the economy across income, savings, expenditure and lending. To make it easier for clients to benefit from the award-winning reports produced by our team, we have unlocked access to all Economic Insights . Economic Insights no longer require a login or password and you will be able to view reports from our Chief Economist, access Australian and global economic perspectives from our team of experts and read our work on sustainability. ## The Team
The Global Economic & Markets Research (GEMR) team provides exceptional research and insights.
The GEMR team have extensive knowledge and experience in their related fields, including domestic and international economics, foreign exchange, sustainable economics, commodities and bond and rates markets.
