Title: Customer complaints and compliments - CommBank

## Tell us about your complaint
If you're not satisfied with our products, services, staff, complaint handling process, or how we handle your Consumer Data Right (CDR data), it's important we hear about it so we can make things right. We’ll take your complaint seriously, work with you to address your complaint and try to find a solution that's fair and reasonable. At all times, we follow our complaint handling principles .

## Tell us about your compliment
We love to hear about your positive experiences and when someone in our team makes things easier, quicker or special for you. We’ll pass on the compliment and make sure we keep up the good work.
