## What to expect from the application process

## Get your resume ready
This is your first chance to tell us why you’re right for this role.
Be sure to explore our support articles on making a resume that’s right for the role.

## Apply through Workday
Log into or create a Workday profile, then complete the online application.
After completing your application, you’ll be able to review our Employment Privacy Statement.
You’ll be able to review a summary of your application before submitting. Make sure to review your application, then click Submit.

## Phone screening
If your application is successful, one of our Talent Acquisition Partners will call you to discuss your alignment for the role.
You can also let us know of any accessibility requirements you may have as part of the application process, or for the role.

## Interview(s)
Interviews may be virtual or face-to-face with the hiring manager or other members of the broader team. Some roles may require more than one interview.

## Offer & background checks
If you are offered the role, congratulations! The next step will be to complete background check. This is part of our commitment to managing risk for both CommBank and our customers. The checks take at least three weeks. Once they’re complete we’ll agree on a start date for your new role.
