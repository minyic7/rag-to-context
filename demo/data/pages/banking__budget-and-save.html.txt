## Meaningful money goals
We all have goals for the future, as well as the desire to create a financial safety net for ourselves. But modern life can present us with so many spending temptations that don’t fulfil us long term and inevitably take us off course from our savings goals. The right savings account, paired with the CommBank app’s personalised tools and features can help.

## Goal Tracker
Join the 400,000+ Australians 1 who’ve already set a savings goal with Goal Tracker. Choose a goal and we’ll break it down into weekly targets with personalised prompts - so it’s less overwhelming and doesn’t feel completely out of reach. Available with a NetBank Saver or GoalSaver in your name only.
