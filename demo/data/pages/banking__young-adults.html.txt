## $50 Welcome Cashback offer - Frequently asked questions

## How do I qualify for the Welcome Cashback Offer?
To eligible for the Welcome Cashback offer, you must, during the offer period (16 January 2026 to 31 March 2026):
open your first Everyday Smart Access Account online or in branch and
spend $10 AUD (or more) in a single transaction at a selected merchant
Offer is only available to new customers, single account holders, and on their first Everyday account. You must be aged 18 years old or above at the time of account opening.
To be eligible for the cashback, you must not have held a CommBank Credit Mastercard, or CommBank Debit Mastercard, CommBank Business Credit Mastercard or CommBank StepPay digital card at any time after 31 December 2019, to be eligible for the cashback.
Excludes joint account holders and Everyday Offset accounts.

## How do I qualify for the $50 cashback?
You will need to activate the cashback offers in CommBank Yello through the CommBank App, and spend at least $10 in one transaction at each selected merchant using your CommBank Debit Mastercard to receive $10 cashback per merchant. Limit of one offer redemption per merchant, per customer.
Selected merchants are: Chatime, Chemist Warehouse, Cotton On, JB Hi Fi, Uber [Rides only].
Transactions must be settled (not pending) by the end of the offer period to be eligible.
Offer Terms and Conditions & exclusions may apply, for full offer terms and conditions refer to the merchant offer in the CommBank App. This offer is not available in conjunction with any other offers, promotions or discounts offered by the selected merchants.

## How much cashback will I receive?
The total maximum cashback you can receive is $50 (AUD), with a limit of $10 (AUD) cashback per selected merchant.
Cashback is limited to one redemption per merchant, per customer.

## When will I receive my cashback?
Your cashback will typically be credited to your active Everyday Smart Access Account within 14 business days of a qualifying purchase, but in some cases may take longer.

## How will I know that I have received the cashback?
A transaction on your Everyday Smart Access Account will appear as ‘CommBank Yello Cashback Offers’ in the CommBank App and ‘CBCASHBACK’ in Netbank.

## Who is an eligible cashback merchant?
Chatime (In-store & Kiosks and via Chatime app only), this offer excludes Tasmania.
Chemist Warehouse (Online & In-store) - excludes health fund claims and prescriptions.
Cotton On (Online & In-store) - excludes Cotton On Kids, Cotton On Body, Typo, Factorie & Supre. Excludes Department Store sales and any other authorised retailer.
JB Hi Fi (Online & In-store) – excludes JB Hi-Fi Business sales and products sold via third parties (JB Hi-Fi Marketplace)
Uber [Rides Only] (Online) - excludes voucher codes, gift cards, cashback and coupon deals. Excludes Uber Eats, Uber Pass, CarShare and Lime Bike or Scooter hire, Uber Courier, Uber Shops, Uber Grocery & Car Rental. Cashback will not be applied if card used to pay is changed after the order has been placed.

## Am I eligible for the CommBank Yello rewards program?
To participate in the CommBank Yello program, you must meet the eligibility criteria for the CommBank Yello program. Ongoing eligibility conditions apply to CommBank Yello, see commbank.com.au/commbankyello for more information and the full terms and conditions, including the eligibility criteria. Please note that this cashback offer does not qualify you for the CommBank Yello program. You must meet the eligibility criteria for the CommBank Yello program in order to participate in the program.
