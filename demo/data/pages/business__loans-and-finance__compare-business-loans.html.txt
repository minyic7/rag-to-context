## Frequently asked questions

## What should I consider when choosing a business loan?
There are a few things to consider when choosing a business loan, including the loan purpose and the different types of security and interest rates.

## What's the difference between a business term loan and a line of credit?
When you get a business loan, you receive the money upfront and pay it back with interest over an agreed time.
A line of credit works similarly to a credit card – it gives you the option to access a pool of money, but you don't have to use it if you don’t want to. You only pay interest on the amount you use.

## How do I apply for a business loan?
It depends on the type of loan you’re applying for. With most business loans you can apply online, in branch or over the phone. There are a few documents you’ll need when applying for a business loan, so it’s good to have these ready.
