## What is AIA Vitality?
AIA Vitality is a personalised, science-backed health and wellbeing program that supports you to make healthier choices. It helps you understand your current state of health, provides tools to improve your overall health, and offers incentives to keep you motivated on your wellbeing journey. With AIA Vitality, you're rewarded for making healthier choices. You can earn up to $760 every membership year in vouchers from AIA Vitality partners or cash into your CommBank account, including; Up to $260 each membership year for reaching personalised physical activity targets each week; and Up to $500 each membership year when you reach Points milestones on your way to achieving a higher AIA Vitality Status. ## AIA Vitality Starter
When you take out eligible Basic Accident Only Hospital, Basic Plus Hospital or Bronze Hospital policies, you get access to AIA Vitality Starter, our science-backed health and wellbeing program. With AIA Vitality Starter, you can earn up to $260 each membership year for achieving active benefit targets.
With both programs, you start your journey on Bronze Status. As you make healthier choices and earn more Points you’ll move from Bronze to Silver, Gold and finally to Platinum.
You can earn AIA Vitality Points through things like increasing your physical activity or going for health checks. The more Points you earn, the higher your Status, and the greater the rewards.

## How does AIA Vitality work?
You can earn AIA Vitality Points by learning about and improving your current state of health in areas such as nutrition, exercise and mental wellbeing to receive weekly rewards and contribute to your overall AIA Vitality Status (Bronze, Silver, Gold and Platinum). Your Status will determine the level of discount or cashback you’re able to receive on some of AIA Vitality’s rewards including eligible AIA insurance premiums, cashback on eligible Virgin Australia flights and discounted gym memberships with Fitness First, Goodlife Health Clubs and Virgin Active. Know your health AIA Vitality offers a range of online and offline health assessments to help you learn more about your overall health. Improve your health AIA Vitality helps you maintain good health with discounts on gym memberships, fitness devices and more. Enjoy the rewards AIA Vitality keeps you motivated with ongoing rewards including shopping rewards and discounts, entertainment and life insurance premiums or cashbacks on flights.

## How do I access AIA Vitality?
Take out an eligible health insurance , life insurance , income protection , TPD cover , or trauma cover policy to access or attach AIA Vitality. Take out a Basic Accident Only Hospital, Basic Plus Hospital or Bronze Hospital health insurance policy for access to AIA Vitality Starter. Eligibility criteria may apply. 3
Activate your AIA Vitality membership with the email AIA send to you - just set a password.
Start your AIA Vitality journey by downloading the AIA Vitality app from the App Store or Google Play.
