Title: CallerCheck - CommBank

## What is CallerCheck?
CallerCheck allows you to verify whether a caller claiming to be from CommBank is legitimate, by triggering a security message in your CommBank app. It’s our preferred method of verification as a more secure way to complete the identification process.

## Why CallerCheck?
From time to time, we may call about your account(s) or products and services you have with CommBank. It’s important that we both know we’re talking to the right person. Having up to date information is one way to help us keep your accounts safe and protect you from fraud. With an increase in scam and fraud calls, it can be tricky to know whether the person you’re speaking to is really who they say they are. With CallerCheck you can confirm it’s us and know you’re speaking to a legitimate member of the CommBank team. We also use CallerCheck to make sure we’re speaking with the right person when receiving and making calls, before we share any details about your account.

## How to use CallerCheck
CallerCheck can be used for identification on calls you make to us as well as calls we make to you. You'll need to have version 4.37 or newer of the CommBank app installed. If you don’t have this version, we'll proceed with the standard identification process. Make sure that you log into the CommBank app and follow the prompts to verify the call.

## Receiving calls from CommBank
When you receive a call from our CommBank Retail Banking, Business Banking and CommSec teams to your mobile or landline, all you need to do is ask the caller to use CallerCheck to verify their identity. We will then send a notification directly to your CommBank app. If you’re ever unsure that the caller is legitimate, it’s always best for you to hang up and call back on our phone numbers .

## Received a notification but did not make or receive a call?
If you receive a notification but haven’t made a call to or received a call from us, we recommend that you select ‘No’ or ‘No I didn’t get a call’ so you can get in touch with our Fraud team. Your access to NetBank and the CommBank app will also be locked as a precaution.
