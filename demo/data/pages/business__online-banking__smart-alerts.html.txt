Title: Business Transaction Account Alerts - CommBank

## Smart alerts to help you feel in control
Same day settlement 1 smart alerts , so you get notified the same day that your card sales settle
Low balance alert, so you know if you have enough funds to cover your outgoings
High balance alert, so you can transfer any savings to a savings account like our Business Online Saver and earn interest
Overdrawn alert, so you know if your account becomes overdrawn, giving you the opportunity to balance it and avoid potential fees and charges 2
Incoming payment alert, so you know when you get paid. Stay on top of your cash flow.
Outgoing card payment alerts can be set for either Local transactions or Overseas transactions or both, so you know what’s being paid out of your account.

## Using your Smart Alerts
You'll need: The latest version of the CommBank app or access to NetBank A Business Transaction Account New to CommBank? Open a Business Transaction Account to start using Smart Alerts. Turning your account balance alerts on: Log on to NetBank Go to Settings then Business Account Alerts Set your Smart Alerts for your Business Transaction Account You're set to start receiving Smart Alerts. Learn how to set up same day settlement alerts. Turning your overdrawn alerts on: Log on to NetBank Go to Settings then Notification settings View your Overdraw notifications Select Send notifications and save You're set to start receiving overdrawn alerts. Find out what happens when you overdraw your business account .
