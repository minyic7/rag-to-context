## Who can apply
To hold a World Debit Mastercard, you must:
Be aged at least 18 years old;
Have an Australian residential address, or otherwise be subject to exception;
Have an eligible CommBank transaction account; and
Have an existing linked Debit Mastercard .
Eligible CommBank transaction accounts include:
Everyday Account Smart Access
Pensioner Security Account
Everyday Offset Account
Complete Access Account

## Included cover

## Access to international travel insurance3
Travel with peace of mind, with included travel insurance on the World Debit Mastercard for you and your family for up to 21 days every trip.
Make sure to activate your cover before each trip to receive comprehensive cover via NetBank, the CommBank app, or by calling Cover-More on 1300 467 951. You can increase your cover with a range of upgrades if you need extra protection for existing medical conditions, longer trips, adventurous activities, or are aged 80 years or over.
For more information, please refer to included international travel insurance .

## Extra shopping protection3
What’s more, when using the World Debit Mastercard to pay, you’ll get extra cover for your purchases.
With Price Guarantee , you can claim the difference in price on a purchase made if the advertised difference is more than $75.
Purchase Security Insurance provides 90 days of consecutive cover in the event of loss, theft or damage of a wide range of personal goods purchased anywhere in the world.
Extended Warranty Insurance extends up to one year’s extra warranty on most purchases, on top of the manufacturer’s standard Australian warranty.
For full information on insurances, please refer to the Insurances provided by the World Debit Mastercard (PDF).
