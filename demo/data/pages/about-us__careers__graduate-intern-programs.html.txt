## Our Graduate Program

## How it works
Working with us means you’ll be part of a group of diverse thinkers where your contributions can make a difference.
When you join us as a grad, you’ll be a full-time permanent employee. Our varied rotations across our business will keep you curious and develop your skills. At the end of your 12-24 month program, you’ll be supported to find a career that best suits you.
Learn more about our application process

## Locations
We have roles located all over Australia across both metro and regional locations depending on the career pathway you choose. Keep your eyes peeled for when applications open as that’s when our role locations will be available.

## Eligibility criteria
You must be in the final year of a university undergraduate or postgraduate degree or have completed university studies in the last 24 months
You’ve maintained an overall credit average (65 GPA or WAM). Note: If you're applying for Technology: Engineering, you'll need a minimum GPA or WAM of 70.
You’re a permanent resident of Australia, or citizen of Australia or New Zealand at the time of your application

## Key dates
Applications open – Feb/March and July
Assessment Centres - between April to May and September
Program starts - mid-February the following year

## Summer Intern Program

## How it works
Partner with experienced and accessible leaders and work with brilliant teams on real-life projects over 10 weeks. You’ll be given full support and training, and the guidance to help you decide if CommBank is where you want to be. At the end of the internship, you’ll also have the opportunity for early entry into our graduate program the following year.

## Global Markets Summer Analyst Program

## How it works
Our Global Markets Summer Analyst (GMSA) program runs for 10 weeks over the summer and will provide you with hands-on experience in one of our Global Markets teams.
By joining one of our specialist teams, you’ll discover how we provide core global market products to our institutional and corporate clients. From trading and sales activities in financial markets, to encompassing all foreign exchange, commodities, fixed income and interest rate products. Wherever you end up, you’ll be guaranteed a fast-paced, data-driven environment where your contributions make a difference.
As a summer analyst you’ll receive training and networking opportunities with seasoned industry professionals and be supported by a buddy and your team to ensure your success throughout the program.
At the end of the program, you could receive an offer for a full time position in one of our Global Markets teams, once you’ve completed your studies. Please note GMSA does not provide early entry opportunities to our Graduate Program.
Find out more about the Global Markets Summer Analyst Program

## FAQs

## About the Programs

## What are the start dates of the programs and how long do they run for?
The Graduate Program starts in mid-February. The length of each program varies depending on which business area you're interested in, but the majority of our programs run for 18 months. As a graduate, you'll join the program as a permanent employee.
The summer intern program and GMSA program runs for 10 weeks from late November to early February. This includes a break over the Christmas and New Year period.

## What does CommBank look for in Graduates and Summer Interns?
We look for well-rounded candidates who have successfully balanced university, work experience and extra-curricular activities. Other skills essential to working successfully with us include being able to work in a team and the desire to learn and drive your own career. Most importantly, we look for your motivation to work with us.

## How many Graduates and Summer Interns do you take each year?
Our Graduate Program generally takes around 300+ Graduates each year and our Summer Intern Program takes around 150+ Interns each year.

## What locations are you recruiting for?
The majority of our roles are based in Sydney. We do have a number of roles available in other cities and regional areas. Specific locations of our roles will be available at time of application, and you’ll be asked about your location preference, and locations you'd be open to relocating to.

## Are the Programs available in any of CommBank’s international offices?
Our Programs are only available in Australia.

## Is there a Global Markets Graduate program available?
Global Markets graduate roles do not form part of our group-wide Graduate Program. Global Markets Summer Analysts who successfully complete the program may receive an offer for a specific team within Global Markets.

## Application & recruitment process

## I'm a temporary visa holder. Can I still apply?
Unfortunately temporary visa holders are not eligible. Applicants must have Australian or New Zealand Citizenship or Australian permanent residency at the time the application is submitted.

## I'll be finishing my studies shortly after the Graduate Program commences. Can I still apply?
All successful candidates must have completed their studies and be available to commence full time work in mid-February. Unfortunately we're unable to accommodate late applicants and would suggest you apply for the following year's Graduate Program.

## Can I apply for more than one Career Pathway?
You’re only able to select one Career Pathway preference on your online application. To help you make this decision, we recommend you read about our different pathways in detail before applying. If we read your application and think you’re a perfect fit for another area, we’ll give you a call to have a chat.

## When will you be visiting my university?
Please check the Campus events section to find out when we’ll be visiting your university and running events – either face-to-face or virtually.

## What is the recruitment process?
Find out more about our application process , including tips for a successful application.

## What is the recruitment process timeline? When can I expect to hear from you?
We'll keep in touch after each stage of the recruitment process to advise if your application is progressing.
If you don’t receive a confirmation email of your application within 24 – 48 hours of your application being submitted, check your junk folders and set us up as a known email address ( [email protected] ) so you don't miss out on important correspondence throughout the recruitment process.

## Why can’t I see the Career Pathway I'm interested in applying for listed on the Graduate Program application form?
If the Career Pathway you’re interested in is not appearing on the application page, it means the role has been filled by successful Graduates or Summer Interns and is now closed.

## I missed the application window. Will there be another round of hiring for the upcoming Graduate Program?
We generally complete all hiring a year in advance. Should any further opportunities become available, these will be advertised on our website. We’d encourage you to join our Talent Community in the meantime so we can keep you up-to-date with all things happening at CommBank, including any vacant roles.

## What is an expression of interest?
You may see us advertise opportunities as ‘expression of interest only’. This means that we do not have a current role, however if we have one become available we'll consider applicants who applied to the expression of interest. If and when a role becomes available, we’ll be in touch to re-confirm your interest and availability and discuss next steps.

## Can I send a hard copy of my resume or email my resume directly to you instead of applying online?
No. All applications must be submitted online.

## I'm working for CommBank but not on the Graduate Program. Is the application procedure for me different? What do I need to do to apply?
All applicants need to apply online. Internal candidates who are already working for CommBank should apply through the Career Hub on Sidekick. External candidates should apply through our application portal . When applications are open, the portal can be accessed via this page. All applicants will go through the same recruitment process.

## Assessment Centres

## How can I prepare for the Assessment Centre?
It pays to be prepared! Do your homework before the Assessment Centre and find out everything you can about the Program and us. Also, it’s worth reflecting on how your skills and experience match what we’re looking for. You’ll have the opportunity to meet and network with CommBank leaders within the Career Pathway you've applied to, so we recommend you’re prepared to discuss your interest and understanding in this area.

## Do you help financially when travelling from interstate to an Assessment Centre?
Yes. Where you're required to travel to meet with us, we'll assist in subsidising interstate travel to Assessment Centres. Available amounts will be discussed with each individual candidate.

## Do you offer virtual Assessment Centres?
We offer virtual assessment processes where we’re not able to bring our team and our candidates together face-to-face. You'll be advised during the application process on whether you can expect to come through either the virtual or face-to-face Assessment Centre.

## Support

## Do you help financially if I relocate to accept a role?
Yes. We'll provide some assistance to Graduates needing to relocate for the Graduate Program. Available amounts will be discussed with individual candidates.

## What is the starting salary for Graduates?
Our starting salary is competitive with the graduate market. We'll discuss details of the remuneration and bonus structure throughout the recruitment process. We also offer a huge range of rewards and benefits outside of your salary.

## Do you offer support for Graduates to complete further tertiary qualifications and training?
Yes. We support employees who want to enhance their professional development through tertiary study. This is something you'll need to discuss with your manager, as you need to consider the benefit to the business and your ability to manage your work/life/study balance.

## Can I travel overseas with CommBank?
While we do have offices around the globe, the majority of our employees are based in Australia. However, in some cases our Graduates will have the opportunity to work overseas.
