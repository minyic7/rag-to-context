Title: Securitisation - CommBank

## About
Securitisation is used by the Bank for funding, capital and credit portfolio management. The Medallion Trust Program was established by the Commonwealth Bank of Australia in October 1997 to enable the securitisation of its own assets. The Medallion Master Trust Deed provides for the establishment of an unlimited number of trusts. Each series trust is separate and distinct from any other trust established and the assets of each are not available to meet the liabilities of any other series trust. You can access up-to-date performance information here for the benchmark Medallion securitisation programs as well as similar details for the former Swan, CATS and Goldstar transactions. For any Bloomberg clients, further information on Commonwealth Bank of Australia (CBA4 <Go>) and Medallion Trusts (MEDL <Mtge><Go>) can be accessed.
