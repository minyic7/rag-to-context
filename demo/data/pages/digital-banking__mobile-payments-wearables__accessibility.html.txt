## Explore the range of options to help you pay, your way
Your smartphone can replace your credit and debit cards, so there’s one less thing to worry about when paying when you’re out and about. It’s secure and easy to use your phone to make payments.
Use the CommBank app to Tap & Pay, or choose from our range of digital wallets - like Apple Pay or Samsung Pay. Simply link your eligible CommBank debit or credit card to the app to get started. 1

## How to make a contactless payment using Tap & Pay
Ready your device based on your selected payment method (i.e. wake the device, unlock the device, or open the CommBank app on your device) Hold or tap your device near the payment terminal Wait for the payment to be confirmed by the terminal (usually within a few seconds)
Ready your device based on your selected payment method (i.e. wake the device, unlock the device, or open the CommBank app on your device)

## How to set up Samsung Pay (for people who are blind or have low vision)

Pay with your Galaxy smartphone using TalkBack and Samsung Pay. Follow these steps to set it up:

## Step 1
Enable TalkBack. Go to ‘Settings’, tap ‘Accessibility’ and then tap ‘TalkBack’.
Go to ‘Settings’, tap ‘Accessibility’ and then tap ‘TalkBack’.

## Step 2
Sign in to your Samsung account. Open the Samsung Pay app on your home screen by double-tapping the app icon. ​ ​ A ‘Permissions for Samsung Pay’ screen will appear. You’ll need to allow access to your Phone, Contacts, Camera and Location. Double-tap the ‘Continue’ button and then double-tap the ’Allow’ button to accept each permission.
Sign in to your Samsung account.
Open the Samsung Pay app on your home screen by double-tapping the app icon. ​ ​ A ‘Permissions for Samsung Pay’ screen will appear. You’ll need to allow access to your Phone, Contacts, Camera and Location. Double-tap the ‘Continue’ button and then double-tap the ’Allow’ button to accept each permission.

## Step 3
Confirm your verification method. The ‘Get started’ screen will appear. Swipe up with 2 fingers to continue. To verify your identity, you can use your fingerprint by double-tapping ‘Use fingerprint’. Or, you can double-tap ‘Skip and use Samsung Pay PIN’ instead to enter a Samsung Pay PIN only.​ To authenticate your fingerprint, you’ll still need to register a Samsung Pay PIN. You’ll be asked to enter this twice. Double-tap to confirm.

Verify your bank card. ​ ​You’ll receive a one-time passcode via SMS, or you can verify through the CommBank app.​ ​ Enter the code and then double-tap ‘Submit’.​ Your CommBank card is now activated.

## Things you should know
1 You can add your CommBank Debit Mastercard, CommBank World Debit Mastercard™, personal and business credit cards, and Visa Business Debit card. If you are an additional cardholder, you may add your eligible Commonwealth Bank credit card provided it is Mastercard branded. Visa credit cards issued to additional cardholders may be eligible, however, a transaction limit of $100 will apply. Some CommBank cards are ineligible including Keycard, Travel Money Card, Mastercard Business Debit Card, Corporate Card.
