## How to apply
Who can apply? You can apply for a Pensioner Security Account if you: Are at least 55 years old; and Have an Australian residential address Existing customers can apply online . The account must be in personal names. Joint accounts (where applicable) can include up to a maximum of two account holders. If you are a new customer or don’t bank online with us, you can apply at your nearest branch . You'll need: To provide your tax residency ( a Government requirement ) You may choose to quote your Tax File Number (TFN), Australian Business Number (ABN) or TFN Exemption. 7
