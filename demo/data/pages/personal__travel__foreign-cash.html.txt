## Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I cancel or change my online order?
The online Foreign Cash service is delivered by our partner Travelex 3 . Once you have submitted an order and made payment, it cannot be changed or cancelled.
If you no longer need your foreign currency order you can sell the following foreign currency notes at select CommBank branches: USD, EUR, GBP, NZD, JPY, CAD, SGD, HKD, CNY. View our current foreign exchange rates (refer to the ‘Change foreign cash’ column for our sell rates). This service is only available to existing CommBank customers.
Selling foreign currency to CommBank will incur a 1% service fee (minimum $10 per transaction). Any commission, fees, or taxes that you paid on your original Online Foreign Cash order will not be refunded.
Use the CommBank branch locator to find branches that will accept foreign cash. Remember to filter branches by the ‘Foreign cash’ option.
If you need to contact Travelex, call 1800 815 947 . Make sure you have your reference number handy.

## How can I check current exchange rates?
View our current foreign exchange rates .

## Does CommBank charge a fee for foreign cash orders?
Yes, CommBank charges a 1% transaction fee (minimum $10 per transaction) for both online and in-branch transactions.

## Can I specify the currency denominations or notes I receive?
No, Foreign Cash orders are packed as a mix. You’ll receive a mix of small and large value notes, depending on availability.

## Are there restrictions on the amount of currency I can bring in and out of a country?
Some countries have restrictions on the import and export of local and foreign currencies. Before placing an order, we recommend that you visit official government websites, including those of your destination countries, or contact their consulates for more information.

## What is the minimum and maximum amount I can order online?
For online orders, you can order a minimum of AUD$500 and maximum total value of AUD$10,000 of all available foreign currency transactions in any 24-hour period. Orders are also limited to a maximum of AUD$20,000 over a 21-day period.

## What foreign currency banknotes can I sell back to CommBank?
If you’re a CommBank customer, you can sell USD, EUR, GBP, NZD, JPY, CAD, SGD, HKD and CNY currencies at select CommBank branches. Check our CommBank branch locator to find branches that can buy back foreign currency banknotes. Please note we’re unable to buy back foreign currency coins.

## I’m not a CommBank customer. Can I purchase foreign cash from CommBank?
Yes, you don’t need to be a CommBank customer to place an online order.

## I’m not a CommBank customer, can I sell foreign currency banknotes back to CommBank?
No, this service is available only for CommBank customers.

## I urgently need foreign cash today. What are my options?
If you’re a CommBank customer, you can buy up to 9 currencies over the counter at select CommBank branches. Check our CommBank branch locator to find branches that sell foreign currency. Remember to filter branches by the ‘Foreign cash’ option.

## How do I lock in an exchange rate when I order foreign cash online?
When you make an online order, you purchase foreign currency and lock in the retail foreign exchange rate at the time of the conversion when the order is submitted. By doing this, you won’t be impacted by any changes to the exchange rate after the conversion has taken place.

## Once I order and pay for my Foreign Cash online, when will my online order be ready for collection?
Your online order will be ready for collection in 5 business days if you place your order and payment is received before 2.30pm (Syd/Melb time). Business days are Monday to Friday, excluding Public Holidays and Bank Holidays in New South Wales. An additional business day is needed for orders made after 2.30pm (Syd/Melb time).

## Can I collect my online order from any CommBank branch?
Yes, during the online ordering process, you’ll be asked to choose a CommBank branch to collect your online order. You can nominate any of our CommBank branches for your collection.

## How long in advance can I place an order for collection?
You can order your Foreign Cash up to 21 calendar days in advance.
