Title: Buy a home

## What is Buy a home?
Buy a home in the CommBank app gives you access to guidance, tools and insights to help you throughout your home buying and ownership journey.
From the moment you start saving for your home loan, Buy a home can help you through each step of the home buying process. From understanding your borrowing power and exploring property estimates, to tracking your application – all in the convenience of the CommBank app.

## Features & benefits
Access to Home Lending Specialists Get access to personalised support at every step of your home buying and ownership journey. Track your application in real time The Application Status Tracker in Buy a home makes it easy to instantly resume  and track your application at any time, providing transparency through the home buying process. Home-in Unlock fast contract reviews through Buy a home and be ready to bid in as little as six business hours. Explore products ​ Discover CommBank’s range of home loan products, including their features, rates and fees. Property Insights Search for you dream property to get estimates provided by Commbank (Listing data provided by Cotality) Tool & calculators A one-stop shop for our popular home loan calculators and tools to help you along the way, such as our borrowing calculator, upfront cost calculator and repayment calculator.

Search for ‘Buy a home’ or find it on the app’s dashboard screen.
