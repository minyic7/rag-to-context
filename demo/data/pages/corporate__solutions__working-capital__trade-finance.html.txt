## Frequently asked questions

## What is working capital?
Working capital is the amount of cash and liquid assets a business has on hand to meet its current and short-term expenses. Learn more about why working capital is important .

## How do I choose the right product for my business?
We’ll work with you to find a complete banking solution that suits your business needs. Call us on 13 1998 (select option 3, then option 5), to speak with a specialist who will help analyse your supply chain and come up with the best working capital products for your business.

## Is working capital finance a business loan?
Yes, working capital finance is a loan designed to help cover the costs of your business’ day-to-day operations.

## What type of business is suited to working capital products?
Our working capital solutions are ideal for businesses with a minimum 12 months trading history who are buying and selling goods or services domestically and overseas to multi-national corporations or manufacturing large amounts of inventory globally.

## How much does it cost for a working capital product?
This is based on assessment of your business, financial records, loan amount and type of facility. Our prices are competitive and some of our trade products can be funded in the currency of your invoices, providing a natural hedge against exchange risk.

## Who can benefit from working capital products?
A wide range of businesses can benefit, especially manufacturing, wholesale distribution and transport and logistics. These industries often experience cash flow challenges due to timing mismatch between expenses and revenue generation to finance their day to day operations.
