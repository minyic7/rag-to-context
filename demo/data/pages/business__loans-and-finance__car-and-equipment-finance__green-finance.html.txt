Title: Green Vehicle and Equipment Finance

## Financing solutions to fund your investment in green vehicles and equipment
CommBank is proud to support Australian businesses to invest in new and used electric and hydrogen vehicles and equipment, and generate their own renewable energy. Switching to green assets can support your business’ sustainability goals and may lead to energy cost savings. We offer financing solutions specifically to fund your investment in green vehicles and equipment that cover 100% of the purchase price and supporting infrastructure such as chargers and batteries.
