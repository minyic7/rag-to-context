## News and updates
Annual Members' Meeting On Thursday 11 December 2025, Avanteos Investments Limited (AIL) hosted the Annual Members’ Meeting for the 2025 financial year. AIL (referred to as Colonial First State or CFS) is the Trustee for Essential Super and the issuer of interests in Essential Super. The meeting was held online and was open to Essential Super members. Attendees heard from our Board and Executive Team about what we’ve achieved over the 2025 financial year, what we’re focusing on moving forward and what this means for members. It was also an opportunity for members to ask questions of our team. If you couldn’t make the event, don’t worry. The video recording, transcript, minutes and Q&As will be available on the CFS website from 22 December. Simply visit the CFS Annual Members’ Meeting page .

## Accessing your super
Super is a long-term investment for your retirement, but there are some circumstances where you may be able to access your super early. Check your eligibility based on: Severe financial hardship Compassionate grounds Terminal illness or permanent incapacity Permanently leaving Australia The First Home Super Saver scheme For all other types of withdrawal, please call 13 4074 from 8:30am - 6pm (Syd/Melb time), Monday to Friday.
