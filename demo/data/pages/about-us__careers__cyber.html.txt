## Cyber Security Technology teams
Our teams are responsible for providing best in class security, resiliency, and reliability. They help keep our people and customers safe sound and secure whilst helping us maintain our position as Australia's most trusted Bank.
Check out the teams making this happen and see where you might fit in.

## Identity Access Technology team
We ensure the availability and delivery of multiple enterprise platforms including authentication services, identity governance administration and privileged access management.
As part of the team, you’ll play a key role in expanding Identity Access Management services throughout the Group. Your work will help create enterprise-wide solutions that allow the bank to operate efficiently while ensuring security. Additionally, you’ll focus on improving employee experiences through automation and self-service capabilities, ensuring appropriate access is granted to the correct individuals at the right times.
Here you'll work on large, complex, and interesting problems that touch almost all aspects of the Bank’s operations.

## Group Security and Engineering
As a member of our team, you'll have the opportunity to design and build enterprise-grade platforms and products that are changing the game in cyber, fraud, and protective security.
We're investing in newly emerging, cutting-edge technologies that will revolutionise the way we run safe, sound, and secure tech. Our mission is to strengthen the security of our cyber platforms used by over 50 thousand of our colleagues and 17 million customers.
You'll have access to technologies at scale, with opportunities to experience a range of roles and domains that will accelerate your career. With millions of people relying on us, your work will make a real difference in protecting the data of our people, our customers, and the community.

## Cyber Defence Operations
Our team is the first and last line of defence against cyber attacks and cybercrime.
As part of our team, you'll be responsible for detecting and responding to cyber threats in real-time. You’ll use market-leading technologies to perform real-world red, purple, threat hunt, and OSINT team testing to stay ahead of the game.
You'll also play a critical role in supporting the Group meet its cyber regulatory and compliance requirements by providing cyber-attack and vulnerability assessments against our assets.
This is a highly technical and exciting space to work in. Cyber attackers are always changing their tactics, so your role will be constantly evolving to stay one step ahead. You'll be joining a sophisticated team with advanced tooling and the resources needed to keep data safe.

## Cyber Governance, Compliance, Education and Outreach
Our team are responsible for maintaining the Group Information Security Policy Framework and governing exceptions against the requirements. We conduct compliance assessments in relation to regulatory and industry security obligations, respond to enquiries from our investors and clients, and provide security reporting into our governing bodies.
As part of this team, you'll manage engagements with business and external stakeholders, including regulators, to ensure our information security practices are aligned with industry standards and regulations.
You'll also make a real impact through educating our people on their cyber security accountabilities and provide cyber security education and awareness to customers and the community through collaboration with industry and government partners.
Here you'll have the opportunity to shape, influence and support the cyber literacy of the Australian population.

## Cyber Intelligence, Resilience and Recovery
Our team enables cyber security to be increasingly intelligence driven. We stay informed about the latest changes in the threat environment, including cyber-attacks against our supplier and partner ecosystem and feed this information to our cyber and business teams to help keep the Group and our customers safe.
As part of the team, you'll be responsible for scanning the external environment for threats. Working closely with our partners in industry and government you’ll ensure proactive management of cyber risks and help the Group be prepared to respond and recover from cyber security events, including data breaches and incidents at our third parties. Your work will ensure the security of our information assets and help us maintain a standout reputation across the Group and market.
You will play a critical role in protecting the Group from cyber threats and maintaining the trust of our customers and stakeholders.
