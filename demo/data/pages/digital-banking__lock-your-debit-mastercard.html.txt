## Secure your account. Manage your spending
Securely lock your CommBank Debit Mastercard or your CommBank Business Visa Debit Card when you need to and unlock it again when you don’t. Use it to help minimise your spending while you’re working out the best financial decisions for you.
Temporarily lock your card Misplaced your card or had it stolen? Add a temporary lock while you look for it and unlock your card if you find it
Lock contactless payments Prefer not to tap & pay with your card? Then add a lock to only pay using your PIN
Lock online payments If you’re not shopping online regularly, add a lock to stop online payments from your card
Lock in-store international payments Spending too much while you’re overseas or staying in Australia for a while? Lock your card to stop international in-store payments.
Gambling lock You can apply a gambling lock to each of your eligible cards. This will automatically block most gambling transactions.

## Lock in the CommBank app
You’ll need to have the CommBank app installed on your phone Log on securely to the CommBank app & tap Cards on the home screen Swipe to the card you want to lock and tap on the image Choose to which lock to activate Log off once you’re done

## You’ll need to have the CommBank app installed on your phone
Log on securely to the CommBank app & tap Cards on the home screen
Swipe to the card you want to lock and tap on the image
Choose to which lock to activate
Log off once you’re done

## Lock in NetBank
If you’re not already registered for NetBank , you can register now Log on securely to NetBank & click on Settings Click  on Lost, stolen or damaged cards in the Card security & limits section Click on Manage beside Debit Mastercard and choose which lock to activate Log off once you’re done
If you’re not already registered for NetBank , you can register now
Log on securely to NetBank & click on Settings
Click  on Lost, stolen or damaged cards in the Card security & limits section
Click on Manage beside Debit Mastercard and choose which lock to activate
Log off once you’re done

## Things you should know
Locks don’t apply to transactions that are flagged as recurring (e.g. direct debits) and transactions not sent to us for authorisation.  Transactions made using Tap & Pay set up on a compatible smartphone aren’t blocked. Some transactions made in CommBank branches, telephone, online or mobile banking including transfers to other accounts, bill payments, and assisted cash withdrawals are also excluded. Other conditions apply. Full terms and conditions available on the CommBank app. The CommBank app is free to download however your mobile network provider charges you for accessing data on your phone. Find out about the minimum operating system requirements on the CommBank app page . Terms and conditions are available on the app. NetBank access with NetCode SMS is required.
