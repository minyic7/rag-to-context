## Message us 24/7 in the CommBank app
Our virtual assistant Ceba can help with everyday banking tasks. Simply tap on help and ask a question:
“I don’t recognise a charge” – follow the prompts to investigate or dispute the transaction
“Lost credit card” – Ceba can help you lock or cancel your card and order a new one
“Where are my bank statements?”– Ceba can take you there
“Change my mobile number” – We’ll message you know when it’s updated or if we need more information
“I’m travelling overseas” – We’ll keep an eye out for any suspicious transactions.
If you need more help, Ceba can connect you by phone or messaging to our customer service team.

## Bank@Post
Our partnership with Australia Post means you have access to over-the-counter banking services at over 3,400 Bank@Post Australia Post outlets. Australia Post staff adhere to strict security and cash management practices, so it’s safe and secure to use your CommBank cards to deposit and withdraw cash. At Australia Post you can: Deposit cash or cheques Withdraw money for personal or business accounts Repay credit cards CommBank customers can also use Australia Post’s ‘Cash Exchange’ service of up to $1,000 at participating post offices (a flat fee applies). More about Bank@Post
