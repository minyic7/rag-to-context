## Frequently asked questions

## What are the benefits of financing business cars and equipment?
Financing business cars and equipment allows businesses to:
Access new or used equipment and vehicles without having to pay upfront
Manage cash flow as payments are spread over a period
Free up equity in other assets as the loan is secured to the financed equipment or vehicle

## Why negotiate your finance and vehicle separately?
When you're buying property, you would usually negotiate the price of the property separately to your mortgage finance. It’s the same with car and equipment finance.
Make sure you’re getting the best deal on both by discussing the prices as separate quotations. We have a range of monthly repayment options and will show you all the finance costs up front to customise a repayment that best suits your business cash flow.

## What are the options for used car financing?
Finance solutions for used cars and equipment include:
Equipment loan: Also known as chattel mortgages, the asset is used as security for the loan and is structured like a mortgage
Hire purchase: Gives you immediate use of the vehicle or equipment while paying it off over a fixed term. Ownership is transferred to you after the last payment
Finance lease: Rent the vehicle or equipment your business needs and return it at the end of your agreement
Read more about the types of car and equipment finance .
