## Frequently asked questions

## What is a Travel Money Card?
A CommBank Travel Money Card is a prepaid Visa ® card that you can load with Australian Dollars and certain foreign currencies before and during your trip. It works like a debit card and you can use it in-store, over the phone, online, or at ATMs. You can lock in exchange rates for budget certainty and manage your funds on-the-go via the CommBank app or NetBank.

## How do I avoid international transaction    fees?
Use a CommBank Travel Money Card to pay with $0 international transaction fees, no load fees or monthly account fees. You'll also avoid surprise exchange rate movements by preloading currencies before you travel for budget certainty.
Some merchants may offer to convert your payment into your home currency and charge high fees for this service. This is called Dynamic Currency Conversion, and it often comes with extra charges. If given the option, always choose to pay in the local currency. This helps you avoid unnecessary merchant fees.

## What card should I consider for international travel?
The CommBank Travel Money Card is a simple and secure prepaid card that makes it easy to manage your travel budget while you're travelling. You can lock in exchange rates for a wide range of currencies, pay no international transaction fees, access 24/7 emergency support and use it everywhere Visa is accepted.

## Can I use my debit card for overseas travel?
All debit cards have different terms and conditions for use overseas. For information about CommBank Debit Mastercard see Debit Mastercard - CommBank .
An alternative is a CommBank Travel Money Card which is prepaid card that's accepted worldwide and is specifically designed to make travel easier. You'll get two cards (one for back-up), emergency 24/7 support, instant card lock/unlock via the CommBank app and pay no international transaction fees.
