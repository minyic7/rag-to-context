## Using ATMs overseas
Before you take off on your overseas adventure, it’s important to plan how you’ll get money out and pay for things. (And don’t forget to let us know - we'll keep an eye out for any suspicious account activity).
You can use ATMs overseas. You’ll get charged every time you withdraw cash from an overseas ATM using your Debit Mastercard, Travel Money Card or credit card though. Unlike ATMs transactions in Australia – they aren’t free.
