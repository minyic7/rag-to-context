## What are Pocket ETFs?
Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) trade on the stock market like individual shares. They let you invest in a group of companies, assets or industries in one simple trade. This can help you spread your risk, as you’re not relying on the performance of just one company.
CommSec Pocket ETFs simplifies investing by providing 10 curated ETF options based on themes like technology, sustainability and top Australian companies. It can also makes investing more accessible with a minimum investment of $50 and low brokerage fees of just $2 per trade (for trades up to $1000 * ).
It’s important to understand the associated risks such as market risk (ETF values can fluctuate), liquidity risk (buying and selling can be hard during volatile periods), performance risk (returns may differ from the underlying index).

## FAQs

## What fees apply to trade CommSec Pocket ETFs?
When you trade through the app, you’ll pay $2 each time you invest or sell up to $1,000. Trades over $1,000 are charged at 0.20% of the trade value. For example, a $1,100 trade will cost you $2.20 ($1,100 x 0.20%).
Late settlement fee of $10 may apply if at time of settlement there are insufficient funds.

## Am I eligible?
You may be eligible to open a CommSec Pocket account if you:
Are 18 years or older with an Australian residential address
Have a CommSec ID or Netbank client number
Have an eligible CommBank bank account (CDIA, Streamline Basic, Cash Management Call Account, Cash Investment Account, Smart Access, Complete Access, Cheque A/c Not Bearing Interest, Cheque A/c Bearing Interest, Private Banking Account, Premium Business Cheque Account, Accelerator Cash Account, Everyday Offset Account)

## What is a settlement account, and why do I need one for my trading account?
A settlement account is a linked bank account used to manage the money going in and out of your trading account. When you buy or sell shares, a process called T+2 settlement takes place where ownership of the shares is exchanged for money between the buyer and seller. This occurs on the second business day after the trade takes place. See ‘Am I eligible?’ FAQ for eligible settlement accounts for CommSec Pocket.

## What is the minimum amount I can invest?
With CommSec Pocket you could start investing with as little as $50 at a time. You can make a one-off investment, or set up regular investments to build your portfolio steadily over time.

## Do I need to maintain a certain investment balance?
There is no need to maintain a certain investment balance.

## What am I investing in?
You’re investing in an Exchange Traded Fund (ETF). ETFs are traded on a stock exchange like listed shares.

## What if I don’t have a Netbank or CommSec ID?
You’ll need to apply online for a CommBank transaction account – this normally takes about 5 minutes. Sign up now Once you have signed up, simply log into the CommBank app, select ‘Investing hub’ from your dashboard or App Library, and select Pocket ETFs.

## Can I use the CommBank app to manage my account?
You can use the CommBank app to trade and manage your CommSec Pocket account. To view your portfolio in the CommBank app Simply log in, and tap the ‘Investing hub’ tile from your app dashboard or Library.

## What returns can I expect from CommSec Pocket ETFs?
Each CommSec Pocket ETF is invested differently and has its own risk and return profile. Performance will depend on how market factors impact underlying holdings i.e. The COVID-19 pandemic had negative impacts on multiple industries generally i.e. travel sector, however certain sectors such as pharmaceuticals had strong growth with increased demand of vaccines and medicines. Be mindful however that past performance is not a good or reliable indicator of future performance.
