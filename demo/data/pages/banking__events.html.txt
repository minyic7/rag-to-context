## Topics
Topics covered in-branch and at our webinars change frequently. Some of the topics we cover are listed below, and you can watch these webinars on-demand by selecting their title. Staying safe online This two-part webinar series is to help you understand some of the ways you can protect yourself online and from scams and fraud. Cost of living tips In this four-part series Jess Irvine, our Personal Finance Expert, shares tips that may help you combat the rising cost of living, budget your money and cut your grocery bills. Financial wellbeing for women You’ll find out about some of the unique financial challenges that women experience and how to overcome them. You’ll also get the tools and information and practical tips to help you build financial independence, become financially secure and enhance your financial wellbeing. Raising financially-savvy kids Helping you understand how to introduce basic financial concepts and the value of money, spending, and goal-setting to kids aged 5-12. Raising financially-savvy teens Information and resources for parents and carers to help support their children’s financial capability, money mindset, and effort associated with earning money for kids aged 13-18. Check back regularly to see our current list of event topics, dates and locations.
