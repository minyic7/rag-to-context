## Discharging for another reason?
You’ll need to complete the commercial discharge form if you’re discharging your business loan because of any of the following: Selling a property Substituting a security (property or cash) Releasing a guarantor Fully or partially repaying your loan You can choose to complete the form online or using a paper form.
You’ll need to complete the commercial discharge form if you’re discharging your business loan because of any of the following:
Substituting a security (property or cash)
Releasing a guarantor
Fully or partially repaying your loan
You can choose to complete the form online or using a paper form.

## Complete online
Complete the commercial discharge form online through DocuSign
Once you’ve completed the form online, we’ll email you a copy to review and submit for signing
Once submitted, we’ll email it to anyone else who needs to sign (e.g. borrower, guarantor) – they can sign the form digitally
Once everyone has signed the form, we’ll start reviewing the request and contact you for any additional information

## Use paper form
Download the commercial discharge form (PDF 1MB)
Complete the form and print it
Ensure all borrowers and guarantors sign the form with a pen
Return the completed form using one of the following methods:
Send it to your Relationship Manager (if you have one)
Email it to [email protected]
Drop it off at your nearest branch

## Important information
Your submission will be reviewed, and a member of our team will contact you within 3 business days to outline the next steps. If you have a Relationship Manager, your request should be referred to them.
Please note that full processing may take up to 15 business days from the date the completed form is received.
Submitting the discharge form initiates the process but does not confirm completion.
Additional documents or information may be required depending on the specifics of the request.
